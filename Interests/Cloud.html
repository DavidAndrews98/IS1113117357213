Andrews Cloud Services

What is Andrews Cloud Services?


We are a leading Cloud Services vendor, who supplies the latest of Cloud offerings to the market. Cloud computing, often referred to as simply "the cloud", is the delivery of on-demand computing resources, everything from applications to data centres, over the internet on a pay-for-use basis.

  • Elastic resources: Scale up or down quickly and easily to meet demand
  • Metered service: so you only pay for what you use
  • Self service : All the IT resources you need with self-service access


Who uses the Cloud?

'Cloud Wars' is a phrase often used to describe the various interactions between the cloud and thousands of the world's leading technology businesses such as Microsoft, Google and IBM.
