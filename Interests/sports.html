Favourite Sport:GAA

Hurling and Football

The GAA is a tradional Irish sport which was founded in 1884. It consists of hurling and gaelic football. Both sports consist of teams of 15 players which line out as seen in the picture below. The objective of the game is to outscore your oppenent. The rules are similar in both codes. One goal is the equilivant of scoring three points. A point is scored by hitting the ball over the cross bar and between the posts.

My Club

I play for a small junior club called Shamrocks GAA. The club was founded in 1984 in the Shanbally/Ringaskiddy/Monkstown parish. We are a small club with about 200 members in total. I've been playing since I was 5 years old.
